🛍️ How to Purchase

  1. Browse through our luxury jewelry collection.
  2. Click Add To Cart on the product you want.
  3. Open your View Cart and review your order.
  4. Click Proceed to Checkout.
  5. Fill in your Full Name, Phone Number, Email and Delivery Address.
  6. Complete your payment securely.
  7. Once payment is confirmed, your order will be processed immediately.
  8. We will contact you and deliver your jewelry as soon as possible.
